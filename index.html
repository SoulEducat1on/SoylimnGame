ABOUT

关于我们

SoylimnGames was born from a shared passion for games and the communities around them. We believe games are more than entertainment — they can carry ideas, emotions, memories, and experiences that stay with people long after the screen turns dark.

SoylimnGames 诞生于玩家社群对游戏的热爱。我们相信，游戏不仅是娱乐，它也可以承载思想、情绪、记忆与体验，在人离开屏幕后依然留下痕迹。

Rather than a traditional studio, SoylimnGames is an open creative collective. We believe meaningful projects can emerge through free collaboration — developers, artists, musicians, writers, and players coming together to create something larger than themselves.

相比于传统意义上的游戏工作室，SoylimnGames 更像一个开放的创作社群。我们相信，真正有生命力的作品，往往诞生于自由协作之中。开发者、画师、音乐人、写作者与玩家，都能够因为共同的热爱聚集在一起，创造出超越个人的作品。

We reject formulaic production and trend chasing. We value experimentation, artistic expression, and experiences that feel personal, unforgettable, and alive.

我们拒绝模板化生产与盲目追逐潮流。我们重视实验、表达与独特体验，追寻那些真正令人难忘的瞬间。

Whether it's a world that continues breathing without you, a mechanic that lingers in your thoughts for days, or a quiet moment shared between player and game — we pursue the kinds of experiences that cannot be manufactured by industrial design alone.

也许是一个无需玩家存在也会自行呼吸的世界，也许是一个在游玩数日后仍令人回味的机制，也许只是玩家与游戏之间某个安静而真实的瞬间。

We welcome creators from different backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives. As long as there is mutual respect, anyone can contribute, collaborate, and become part of the community.

我们欢迎来自不同背景、文化与视角的人参与创作。只要彼此尊重，任何人都可以贡献自己的想法、技术与热情，成为这个社群的一部分。

We believe games can be art. And more importantly, we believe they can connect people.

我们相信游戏可以是艺术。更重要的是，我们相信游戏能够连接人与人。

Built by the crowd. Shaped by experience.

献给芸芸众生。

VALUES

理念

01.

Decentralization

去中心化

We have no fixed hierarchy or central authority. Anyone can start projects and contribute to creation. We believe true creativity is born through free collaboration.

我们没有固定的权力中心。任何人都可以发起项目、参与创作。我们相信自由协作能够诞生真正有生命力的作品。

02.

Games as a Medium

游戏作为媒介

Games are more than entertainment. They can share knowledge, express ideas, and create meaningful experiences.

游戏不仅是娱乐。它也可以传播知识、表达思想、承载情绪与体验。

03.

Anti-Industrial Creation

反流水线化

We reject formulaic production We encourage experimentation, failure, unique artistic expression and market.

我们拒绝模板化创作。允许实验、允许失败、允许独特表达、允许不迎合市场。

04.

Open Spirit

开放精神

Regardless of education, nationality, culture, or beliefs, anyone can participate as long as they respect others and the community.

无论学历、国家、文化背景或立场，只要尊重他人与社群，任何人都可以参与创作。
