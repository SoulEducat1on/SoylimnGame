ABOUT

关于我们

We believe games are more than entertainment — they are living worlds, emotional journeys, and shared memories. At SoylimnGames, every project begins with a single question: "What will the player feel?"

SoylimnGames 成立于对游戏创作的热爱。我们相信游戏不仅是娱乐，更是鲜活的世界、情感的旅程与共同的记忆。在每个项目中，我们都以同一个问题作为起点：「玩家会感受到什么？」

We are a small, passionate team. And we like it that way. Staying small means every detail matters — every pixel, every line of dialogue, every note of music. We don't ship until it feels right.

我们是一支小而精的团队。小，意味着我们在意每一个细节——每一个像素、每一句对白、每一个音符。在作品达到应有的「感觉」之前，我们绝不交付。

Our approach blends handcrafted artistry with thoughtful design. We look beyond genre conventions and chase the spark that makes a game unforgettable — whether it's a world that breathes without you, or a mechanic you can't stop thinking about days later.

我们的理念是将手工艺般的匠心与深思熟虑的设计相结合。我们超越类型框架，追寻那些让游戏令人难忘的火花——无论是一个无需你存在也会自行呼吸的世界，还是一个让你在游玩数日后仍会不断回味的机制。

We are committed to creative independence. No chasing trends. No compromise on vision. Every SoylimnGames title carries a piece of who we are — our culture, our stories, and our unwavering belief that games can be art.

我们坚守创作独立。不追逐潮流，不妥协愿景。SoylimnGames 的每一款作品都承载着我们的一部分——我们的文化、我们的故事，以及我们对「游戏可以是艺术」的坚定信念。

We don't make games to fill time. We make games to leave a mark.

我们创作游戏，不为消磨时间，而为留下印记。

VALUES

理念

01.

Craft Over Scale

匠心重于规模

Every project is a craft — a hand-shaped thing. We'd rather make one unforgettable experience than a dozen forgettable ones.

02.

Feel First

感受优先

Mechanics, visuals, and sound all serve one master: emotion. If it doesn't make you feel, we go back to the drawing board.

03.

Cultural Roots

文化根源

We draw from stories, aesthetics, and perspectives that haven't been explored enough in games — and we bring them to a global audience.

04.

Player Respect

尊重玩家

No dark patterns. No time-wasting padding. We respect your time, your intelligence, and your trust. A game should earn every hour you give it.
